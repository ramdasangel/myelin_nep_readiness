KEY FINDINGS
=============

1. Both leaders and teachers show highest alignment with FP2 (Holistic & Experiential Learning)
2. FP1 (Each Child is Unique) shows lower scores, indicating an area for development
3. Parent-Teacher Collaboration (FP5) varies significantly across respondents
4. There is substantial variation within both groups, suggesting different readiness levels

RECOMMENDATIONS
================

1. Targeted Training: Focus on FP1 (recognizing individual learner differences)
2. Peer Learning: Create communities of practice around high-scoring FPs
3. Leadership Development: Align leader and teacher orientations
4. Continuous Monitoring: Conduct quarterly assessments to track progress
